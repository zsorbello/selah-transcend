Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Selah ("we," "our," or "the app") is a faith-rooted mental wellness companion built for personal reflection and emotional clarity. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address (for login and cloud backup)
  • Onboarding responses (name, age range, faith level, focus areas)
  • Journal entries, mood logs, and session reflections
  • Assessment responses
  • Feedback submissions

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data (streak counts, session counts, feature engagement)
  • Device type and browser for app compatibility
  • Push notification subscription tokens (if enabled)

Payment information:

  • Payments are processed entirely by Stripe. We never see, store, or have access to your credit card number, bank details, or full payment information. We only receive confirmation of your subscription tier and email address from Stripe.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the Selah experience — AI reflections, anchors, assessments, and personalized content
  • To save and restore your data via cloud backup
  • To send login verification codes
  • To process and verify subscription payments
  • To send push notifications you've opted into
  • To improve the app based on aggregated, anonymous usage patterns

3. AI-Powered Features

Selah uses Anthropic's Claude AI to power reflection sessions, daily anchors, assessments, and personalized recommendations. When you use these features, your relevant context (such as session topic, onboarding answers, or faith level) is sent to the AI to generate responses. AI conversations are not stored by the AI provider beyond the immediate request.

AI responses are not reviewed by licensed professionals and should not be treated as medical, psychological, or clinical advice.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable cloud backup, your data is encrypted and stored securely via Upstash Redis with a 90-day rolling retention window that refreshes each time you save.

We use industry-standard security practices, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Period.

We share data only with:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — to generate AI-powered responses during your sessions
  • Stripe — to process subscription payments
  • Resend — to deliver login verification and notification emails
  • Vercel — to host and serve the application

Each of these providers has their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

6. Children's Privacy

Selah is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. For users aged 13–17, we strongly encourage parental awareness and may require parental consent through our in-app consent system.

If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has used Selah, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all your data is visible to you within the app
  • Delete your data — use "Reset All Data" in Settings to erase everything locally, or contact us to remove cloud backups
  • Opt out of notifications — toggle push notifications off in Settings
  • Cancel your subscription — manage or cancel anytime via the Stripe customer portal in Settings

8. Cookies & Tracking

Selah does not use cookies, third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or fingerprinting. We do not serve ads. Your experience is private.

9. Data Retention

Local data persists until you clear it or reset the app. Cloud backup data is retained for 90 days from your last save, then automatically expires. Payment records are maintained by Stripe according to their retention policies. Login session tokens expire after 30 days of inactivity.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be noted in-app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
